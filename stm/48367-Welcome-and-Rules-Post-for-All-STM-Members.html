    Welcome and Rules Post for All STM Members

    Hi there! On behalf of the admins and moderators team, please allow me to extend to you a warm welcome to the STM community!

    (And if you're a vendor who is representing a business, please also read this post in addition to the current post - it contains tips on how you can promote your business without breaking forum rules.)

    This post will help you to:

    1)Familiarize yourself with the Forum Rules

    2)Get the most out of STM

    3)Know how to contact us!



    ***************

    Forum Rules

    Please read through the complete list of forum rules which you can find here.

    Here are some of the highlights:

    -Please treat each other with respect, and avoid discussing anything illegal.

    -Buying or selling of Facebook/Google ad accounts is prohibited.

    -Group buys of any sort are not permitted.

    -No affiliate links allowed in forum posts - they are allowed in your signature.

    -Sharing your STM account access with anyone else is not permitted, unless you're a vendor sharing access with a limited few colleagues from the same company.


    Regarding Warnings and Account Bans:

    In most situations, when mods/admins find a member breaking a forum rule, we would send them a warning via PM. Breaking a forum rule a second time (the same or a different rule as the first offence) can result in a permanent account ban. We refer to this as the "two strikes and you're out" rule.

    If an offence is deemed serious, a permanent account ban may be issued without prior warning.

    Ultimately, forum moderators and admins reserve the right to move/delete posts, issue official warnings, suspend a user or permanently ban a user from the forums, at their discretion, and at any time.


    Regarding Spamming:

    If you're a vendor: Please be careful how you promote your business, and in which subforums. Please find details in this post.

    For all members: If you've received an unsolicited PM from anyone who is trying to promote a product or service (unless you've requested for recommendations of such products/services in a forum post or PM), please report the member by emailing support@stmforum.com, with a screenshot of the PM.



    How to Get the Most Out of STM

    Quick Intro of Major Subforums:

    If you have absolutely no experience in running paid traffic campaigns, the $1 guide followed by the 40-day pop tutorial would be the perfect start!

    If you have a bit of experience, or wouldn't mind having to figure some things out for yourself, do check out some of the other tutorials in the Newbie Zone (e.g. Twinaxe's Push Tutorial, or my FB Tutorial or Outbrain Tutorial).

    As a newbie, the best way to learn is to start a follow-along thread to get feedback from more experienced members. There's the 40-Day Tutorial Follow-Alongs section, the new Newbie Push Follow-Alongs section, and some of the Traffic Sources subforums each has their own follow-along sections.

    We have a tool you can use to apply to 10+ affiliate networks at once: Affiliate Network Application Tool

    If you want to find contact information for affiliate networks, traffic networks, tooling services etc., do a search in our Affiliate Contacts Directory.

    Do take advantage of all the goodies in the STM Treasure Chest!

    Many affiliates don't realize the power of networking! This has got to be one of the most under-valued activities, and STM is the best place to do this at! Below are some sections specific for networking, but I can't overstate the importance of just participating in discussions in general - giving and receiving information and sharing experiences is the BEST way to make friends!

    Sections for networking: Affiliate World Conferences, STM Local Networking Events, Other Events and Networking, Masterminds

    If you need motivation: Success Stories

    Forum subsections for every type of paid traffic: Traffic Sources

    Wanting to buy/sell a product or service? Check out the STM Marketplace!

    If you're looking to hire or to get hired: Outsourcing and Employment

    I'll leave you to explore the remaining forum sections!


    BBCode:

    You can use BBCode in your posts and signatures to enhance the appearance of your content.
    You can find a good guide on how to use BBCode here.


    How to Find the Latest Posts:

    Click on "New Posts" at the top:




    How to Add a Forum Signature / Profile Picture / Avatar:

    -Click on "Settings" at the top:



    -In the left menu click on "Edit Signature":



    -Add your signature. (BBCode is accepted. Links are also accepted. Banners are not permitted.)

    In the same left menu, you can also find "Edit Profile Picture" and "Edit Avatar". Just click on those and follow the instructions.


    Regarding Forum Search:

    You can find 2 forum search functions at the top:



    The search field is powered by Algolia. The "Advanced Search" is powered by vBulletin. Please try both to see which one you like more. I use both - Algolia for more general searches, and vBulletin for more refined searches (e.g. by user, by subforum, by date range).


    Regarding Asking Questions:

    Mods and admins LOVE to answer any questions you may have on affiliate marketing - this is why we're all here!

    But please know that:

    1)The most common newbie questions have been answered multiple times in multiple threads - please do a search first before asking a question.

    2)If you have zero experience or close to zero experience with paid traffic, DO at least SOME reading in The Newbie Zone so you can ask more specific questions than "how can I make money from paid traffic?" or "what's the best offer/affiliate network/traffic network?"

    We usually don't fault a user for asking questions, unless they ask a bunch of very general questions before even attempting to learn the basics from the newbie tutorials.

    The more specifics you provide in your question, the more efficiently we can answer it.


    Amember:

    At any time, you can visit your amember user control panel at http://stmforum.com/amember - here you will be able to renew subscriptions, change passwords, etc.

    Here is also where you can find your payment invoices for forum subscription - for details please see:

    https://stmforum.com/forum/showthrea...-now-available

    The best way to contact support would be by email:

    support@stmforum.com

    Please also read this post so you'd know how to contact us in case you're locked out of the forums.

    If there's anything else you'd like me to include in this post, please let me know below!

    Again - welcome to STM and we look forward to participating in your journey to success!
