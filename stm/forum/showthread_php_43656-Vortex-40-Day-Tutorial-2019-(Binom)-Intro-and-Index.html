    Vortex' 40-Day Tutorial 2019 (Binom) - Intro and Index

    Having been a newbie myself, and having assisted numerous newbies on this forum and in the 6WAMC course, I know that the biggest cause of inaction is not knowing exactly how to get started, because there is so much decision-making involved.

    Do I get a server? If so where? Which affiliate network? What kind of offers? Which tracker? Which country? Which traffic type/source? Etc. etc...

    This is why many people are stuck in a perpetual state of reading, never reaching the point of taking action.

    So I thought it was about time we tried a different approach - DO FIRST, LEARN WHY LATER.

    If you're learning paid traffic for the first time, and have no idea how to proceed, then this step-by-step course is for you.



    **************************************************

    The course is divided into multiple lessons that will require approximately 40 days to complete. My intention is for each lesson to be long enough so that you will learn something significant from each lesson, but not so long that it would overwhelm you. Most lessons will contain the following sections:


    ACTION - Just follow step-by-step - I've tried to leave out thinking / decision-making as much as possible to avoid procrastination or inaction.


    EXPLANATION - This is where I'll explain the "Why" for what we did in the ACTION section of the lesson.

    Knowing this information, you will be able to make better decisions on your own when you're ready to venture outside of the general rules I've laid out for you in this tutorial - which, while they are the best "one-size-fits-all" solution I can come up with, may not the best approach for every specific campaign.

    It's always more efficient to customize your testing/optimization approach for every individual campaign depending on the particular situation.


    READING - There are TONS of valuable posts on this forum, but many new members are lost in the sea of information, not knowing which posts to read first.

    To remedy this, I will suggest valuable and relevant threads for you to read in some of our daily lessons.



    You may be tempted to just implement the "ACTION" sections and skip the "EXPLANATION" and "READING" sections - please DO NOT DO THAT! Without taking the time to understand WHAT we are doing and WHY we're doing things a certain way, you will not have the necessary knowledge to make your own decisions later. And I certainly won't be around to hold your hand forever!

    If you only implement the "ACTION" sections, you'll end up with a few campaigns at the end of this course, but won't know what to do after that. If you also spend time learning the information delivered in the "EXPLANATION" and "READING" sections, you'll also have the skills required to launch additional campaigns, and and be able to understand more of the numerous other posts in this forum, as you will already have the fundamental knowledge to assimilate the new knowledge into.

    In the end, of course, how much you want to get out of this course is up to you. I hereby leave the decision with you.



    **************************************************

    This course takes almost all of the decision-making out of the process. Within the first few lessons, you will have set up your very first pop-traffic campaign. We will be doing what's called "direct-linking", which means the visitors will be shown popunder ads containing the offer we'll be promoting.

    In later lessons, we'll cover how to show a landing page - aka lander, or pre-lander, or bridge page - to the visitor to pre-sell them first, before sending them to the offer.

    As the Nike slogan goes, "Just do it!" Best of luck, and do post questions below if you get stuck!
