    Some Success & Some Failure

    Alright, so bit of a re-intro. I’m Craig, mid 40’s, a member since 2014 and it’s been well over 3 years since my last post. Figured it was about time to start networking a bit. I haven’t been active on the forum but still gain a lot of valuable info from it. I’ve been doing this thing as a lone wolf…

    So over $6m in revenue the last few years…I know it doesn’t compare to a lot of guys here and yea I’ve done a ton of things wrong, have spent a large amount of hours chasing campaigns and haven’t built an asset, but on the other hand am thankful that I put in the work and managed to get to this point and change my life. Probably should have been smarter and built a team but I always just enjoyed being based in Asia, traveling around and not having the responsibility of employees.

    Was mostly focussed on Nutra on Native…back when Rev Content was good/better. Also Content.ad and Taboola. That was the bulk of my efforts the last few years. But all good things come to an end, and Rev Content changed their ways in early 2018 and the quality also dived (for me and Nutra anyway) and things got more difficult.

    2019 has been a real change, a very tough year, my dad passed away…that’s when everything gets put in perspective. The highs aren’t as high, but the lows are much lower. No coincidence everything else tanked…That aside, I was into Crypto offers,… and also PUSH traffic which is bit of a headache. Some success here and there but not like before. I’ve now experienced bigger loss days/weeks this year than I have before…but it is what it is, you just keep moving on.

    So perspective, things have come full circle.. a downer but hopefully will make me better at this, hopefully new opportunities will open

    For those struggling with getting started or experiencing the downs …you can do it. I’m mid 40’s and really not all that talented at this stuff. I just worked really hard and I had a hell of a lot of fun while doing it. Life events happen & the negative days aren’t easy...but things eventually turn around.

    Where to from here though I’m not sure, it feels like bit of a start over point… maybe try something new, a business partner perhaps…but definitely want to surpass my previous levels!

    In any case, hope to meet some new people and hope everyone is doing great!
