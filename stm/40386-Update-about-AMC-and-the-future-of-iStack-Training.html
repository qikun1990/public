    Update about AMC and the future of iStack Training

    Hi STM

    I took over iStack Training in 2017 and it was a pretty crazy year. We launched Zeno's one and only 6 Week AMC three times through out the year, put on two sold out live training events (Facebook Mastery Live - Berlin and Bangkok), created 10 acceleration modules, hosted an amazing Mastermind in Phuket, as well as running the Facebook Master Class and the Native Ads Master Class. Phew, I'm sweating just thinking about it.

    When we started drawing up plans for 2018, we took a close look at the 6 Week AMC. While it's been updated each time it's run, we decided that it needed a bigger refresh than we'd done in the past. The 6 Week AMC is still the best way to learn the basics of affiliate arbitrage, but after surveying the results of the course takers, and discussing the industry with pros like @Zeno, @BBRock, @CMDeal @MrGreen and @Stackman we found that the opportunity that the course covers (mostly mobile pops, redirects and mobile display) isn't producing the same levels of profit for affiliates. For this reason we've decided to postpone the AMC until we can base it on the best possible opportunity for affiliate marketers. We're definitely open to suggestions if you have any.

    Having had such such an amazing experience with our two live events and our mastermind, we met so many people who were dominating the ecommerce game. Because of this and other industry trends we've decided to focus heavily on ecommerce for our upcoming content. First, as you know, we're hosting Ecommerce Mastery Live on July 20th in Barcelona.

    Further, we've decided to tweak our model a little. Instead of producing several courses, with small to medium launches, with different teachers on different topics spread throughout the year, we're going to focus on launching 1 or 2 BIG courses in as big of a way as possible. We want to focus on impact, distribution and quality as much as possible.

    We're getting ready to announce our next big course project, but I'll tell STM that it will be focused on Ecommerce, will involve several of the speakers from Ecommerce Mastery Live and will be launched at or around Affiliate World Europe.

    If you're keen on either jumping into ecommerce, or rapidly improving your results in this area, this course is going to be well suited for you. We'll be kicking it off with a 6 part free course that will launch in July.

    As we gear up for Barcelona and our big course launch, I'll be continuing the podcast and continuing to put out great content, focused mainly on ecommerce.

    Beyond this summer, plans are up in the air. I love the idea of launching another big course in December on the back of Affiliate World Asia, and it's quite possible that this course/event will return the focus to media buying. My big vision for iStack Training is to provide users the ability choose between two main paths through our content, one where students want to learn to own, run and sell their own products (ecommerce) and ones where they want to promote others' products and services (affiliate/agency).

    As always, we're open to suggestions and always looking for collaborators, so if you have any thoughts on this, please send me a line to eric@istackholdings.com.

    Thanks and stay tuned for news on our All-Star Ecommerce free mini-course soon.

    Best

    -Eric
