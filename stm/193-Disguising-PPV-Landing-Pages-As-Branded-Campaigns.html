    Disguising PPV Landing Pages As Branded Campaigns

    This is a technique that can be applied to any offer/target and can help you create a profitable PPV campaign super quick.

    The Idea
    - For this to work you have to choose 1 target with a fair amount of traffic
    - Essentially this technique is just disguising your landing page as if it's a popup from the target URL itself.

    I recommend doing this the safe and legit way which is making your landing page look like the target URL, by copying colors, shapes, images, fonts etc.
    If you want to go the more blackhat route; you can use logo's and other trademark items, but it's not worth the risk. Copying the design alone without any trademarked items will increase your CTR/CR enough.

    A couple examples
    offer: Walmart Gift Card (email submit)
    target: walmart.com (and subdomains)
    landing page: Walmart is giving away $1000 to the next lucky customers to answer this question. <question goes here>.

    offer: HCG Weight Loss Supplement
    target: JennyCraig.com (and subdomains)
    landing page: JennyCraig is giving away free trials of HCG WeightLoss for free. It's Jenny's newest pro supplement and there's only 14 trials remaining.

    offer: Ringtones (Motime)
    target: Datpiff.com (and subdomains)
    landing page: Datpiff has 40 ringtone packages to give away for Lil Waynes new out of jail CD release. 10 minutes remain, answer this question correctly to get yours! <lil wayne question here>.

    As you can see this can be done with almost any idea. Obviously it will increase conversion rates as trust goes way up. Also it can be scaled horizontally, If it Ringtones (Motime) offer with the idea above profits on Datpiff.com, it's likely it'll work on Eminem.com, and SOHH.com as well (and many many others!). Then you can scale vertically to another PPV traffic source. The only work you have to do is creating landing pages!

    This method isn't exactly 100% legit, but it's not looked at as blackhat either.
    You do need to be selective though with how you do this, your putting yourself in jeopardy doing this with 'identitity theft offers' on 'bank websites', but you have nothing to worry about doing this with IQ quizzes on quiz websites. So just use common sense.

    Overall this method will increase your CTR and Conversion rate, and that's the key to profit.
    - You'll want to use landing pages since that's the whole point of this technique. You can try iframing an offer as well using the same idea.
    - If you find a working landing page/offer, then make a 2nd landing page as well. Rotate your landing pages otherwise you'll never know if you could be making an extra 75% ROI a day.
