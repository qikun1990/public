    First €5k-€8k Profit Day

    Here is my little story how I made €5k-€8k profit on one day - by accident.

    When I started with affiliate marketing in 2005 I was using torrents to drive traffic to my sites.

    Then there was one day in August 2008, I will never forget that day.
    I ran torrents for about 3 years already and made some nice additional money from it.

    On that day I wanted to test something different.

    Then I had some kind of brainwave and decided to test something else as well.

    I didn´t expect much from it, maybe €50-€100 additional per month.

    I created the stuff, uploaded the torrent and then left the house.

    When I was back home few hours later I checked my PC and saw that I already made about €5k or so.

    From one fucking torrent.

    In the end I made about €8k or so on that day.
    I can´t remember the exact amount anymore, it´s almost 11 years ago.

    Unluckily I also can´t access my afiliate account anymore.

    I already tried to reset password but I don´t receive the email.

    When I am able to login there again I will update the post with a screenshot.

    The funny thing is, all the money was pure profit as I was using free traffic.

    At this time I never visited a forum about affiliate marketing, I didn´t use a tracker, I didn´t know what trafficsources or CPA networks are.

    I just had an idea and took action.
