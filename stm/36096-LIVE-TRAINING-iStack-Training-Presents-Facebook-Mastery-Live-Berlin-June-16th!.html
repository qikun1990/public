    [LIVE TRAINING] iStack Training Presents: Facebook Mastery Live - Berlin - June 16th!





    Hi

    Things are happening quickly here at iStack Training, (makers of the 6 Week AMC). Today we're excited to announce a LIVE training event for advanced Facebook Advertisers. The day after AWE, June 16th, iStack Training is partnering with AdBaker, 6HMedia and some other great companies to put on the Facebook training event of the year: Facebook Mastery Live


    At FML we will teach high-level direct Facebook performance marketing know-how & strategies that give attendees an immediate competitive edge. The event is about specific strategies for advertisers, taught by FB marketers who are spending millions on Facebook Ads.

    This is not a day for newbies. Everything taught here is meant to be directly applicable to advertisers who are already spending significant budgets on Facebook


    FML will bring together Facebook marketing masters from several different white-hat areas including agency, CPA, ecommerce, gaming, online education and more. This is not a day for newbies. Each speaker will bring their unique perspective, as well as cutting edge strategies, exploits, case studies and most importantly, results that will both inspire and educate you about the best ways to make millions from the greatest advertising tool ever created.


    Registrations will open later this week, but I wanted to let STMers know about it asap, as we will have some early bird pricing available for this valuable training and I want to make sure everyone from here who wants to come gets a good deal.

    If you're interested make sure you subscribe on iStackTraining.com and we'll reach out as soon as registration opens with early bird options.

    Thanks and see you in Berlin
