    My success story

    Hello!
    My name is Dima, I'm 18 y.o. and I'm from Russia, Kaliningrad.

    A little about me:
    I was born in a poor family. My mother earned about $80 per month and father about $60. I almost did not have toys, we often could not afford something tasty, well, in general, you understand. When I was 6 years old, my dad worked at the dump (recycled polyethylene) and I and my mom went to help him. Dirty, disgusting, stinks, but what to do. But I was never spoiled child. Walking in the supermarket, I was almost never asked for anything, because I knew that my mother last piece gives us. She many years one fed me, brought up, did everything for me and for my dad. Therefore, even though the fact that the three of us lived in a room about 12m, but the house was always clean, and there was an order.

    If we talk about the range of 6-16 years, then there is the most memorable for me - that's what I have at school, in the courtyard, and not only, very spread rot. And I do not even know why. May be because I was poor and weak. I was a good student in school, but I was not an excellent student. But somehow, in school I was faced with more aggression towards me. All laughed at me, called names, and kicked my things, spit on me, in general, when I got up this morning I was thinking only one thing: "I do not want to go there!". Therefore, as early as 9th grade I started skipping school a little bit, just to take a break from it all. I had a little bit friends, so when I was 13 years I became interested in the internet earnings.

    It all started in 2008 when I was 11 years old, and I had no access to the Internet. I lived in a poor family and I had no computer. At that time I was very fond of reading magazines about computers and even collected them. When I bought a computer (Pentium 1), I did not want to play games, and use it for fun. In 2010 I got the internet. I do not know why, but I immediately realized that the Internet can make me money and I can I can work there.

    I started with the creation of websites and forums. First on html, then at Joomla and WordPress. I had no idea of ​​their monetization, but I still had a lot of fun making them, though for a reason. Then I tried Buxes. Oh yeah! I made my first few dollars, but it was terribly tiring, and eventually I gave up. Then I work with "kolotibablo". So I solved captches and I made my 2$ for 2 weeks. I was really happy because I was 14 and it was my first money.



    So I started to make money in the internet. I also made doorways (1 year), I got about $10k. But I wanted more

    In 2013 I started to work with CPA. I bought the traffic and converted it. I worked with banners, teasers and also with Pops. At that time, I was earning $4-5k per month.
    Finally I began to earn the normal amount.

    I worked hard because I lived in a poor family and I wanted to buy iPhone and other good things) When I was 15 I bought my 1st iPhone 5. I was so happy. So so happy!!!

    So I earned my 1st million rubles ($35000) when I was 16 y.o.

    Now I'm working with installs, dating and Pins. I use Pops and banners. I really love my work. Online marketing is my whole life.
    So what is the conclusion I want to make of it all? First of all, do not give up and persevere in your goal. I had a clear goal - to make the Internet a major source of income. Now I do not even think about how to go to offline work. Even if at first you find that everything is very difficult and it is not clear (as it was for me), it's okay. Do you think Bill Gates woke up and immediately become a millionaire? No!

    No one believed in me, and all told me that I did not achieve. It is necessary, many scoffed and laughed at me. But I didn't give up. Now I am 18 years old and I live far away from parents. At this forum, I want to make friends, chat with colleagues. I am very sociable and I can help everyone.




    Sorry for my English

    P.S. My Skype is mrbraun31 and fb is https://www.facebook.com/mrbraun31
