    My Whole Campaign [Test #1] on Adserve.com (Dating Ads)

    This campaign is relating to:
    https://stmforum.com/forum/showthread...rk-Adserve.com

    Adserve.com is a new self serve traffic network for fileserve.com, so i thought I'd give it a run!

    The Interface:


    As you can see there is NO CTR or CPC!! Weird.. So it's VERY hard to optimize your ads and quite confusing on what steps you need to take to improve your campaign

    Offers tested: SpeedDate, True Christian Singles, Mate1

    Ad Screenshot:


    Other 4 Images:


    Suggested Bid Range $1.54 - $1.98 CPM
    Actual Bids: $0.35 CPM - $0.50 CPM
    Best CR Offer: Speeddate (CR of 5.67%)
    Conversions: 26 ($2.90 payout)
    Spent $110
    Profit: $-34 (not bad)

    *I could probably get a payout of $3.30 with some volume which would = $-25$ profit.
    Testing a few more dating offers/networks and a landing page and I'm sure this could be decently profitable!

    - I was able to start bids off at $1.20 CPM, and lowered it by 30cents every hour, with no hit to traffic at all until i got to 0.50 CPM, then traffic slowed a bit. Eventually i got bids to 0.35 CPM while still maintaining good traffic.

    Conclusion:
    Definitely has some serious promise as a traffic source! Especially because competition is VERY low, I'm going to probably try some new offers/landing pages and niches down the road.
