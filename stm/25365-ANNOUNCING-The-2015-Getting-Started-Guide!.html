    ANNOUNCING: The 2015 Getting Started Guide!

    You may have wondered why I've been a bit quiet on the guide-posting front for the last month.

    The reason? I've been working on the 2015 Getting Started In Affiliate Marketing guide. What started as a "let's update the guide" mission became a "let's improve the structure and add some info", which then became less of a mission and more of an EPIC QUEST.

    And the result? It's right here and available to read now. Almost twice as detailed as the original Getting Started Guide, it's designed in a new format that's accessible both as a step-by-step guide AND as a dip-in solution to all your affiliate marketing questions. It includes tons of new material, including an overview of the best strategy to succeed in AM, discussion of a much wider selection of traffic sources than the original guide, a section on pushing up to $x,xxx a day and beyond, and more!

    Here's the full list of what's included:


    • I'm new! What's this "Affiliate Marketing" thing and how does it work?
    • What Software, Hardware and Memberships Do I Need Before I Start My First Campaign?
    • What Skills Do I Need Before I Start My First Campaign?
    • What's The Most Important Factor For Success In AM? (Mindset post)
    • How Much Money Can I Make From Affiliate Marketing, And How Long Will It Take?
    • How Much Money Do I Need To Start Affiliate Marketing?
    • What Area Of Affiliate Marketing Will Make Me The Most Money? What Traffic Source? What Vertical?
    • I Want A Step-By-Step Guide! Where Can I Find One?
    • How Do I Set Up My First Campaign?
    • How To Run A Campaign On Any Traffic Source
    • How To Make Money From Your Campaigns
    • What's The Best Overall Strategy For Success As I Start AM?
    • How Many Campaigns, Offers, And Ads Should I Run?
    • How Do I Know When To Stop Running Ads? What's This "Statistical Significance" Thing?
    • Do I Need To Run Landing Pages? How Do I Create Them?
    • How Do I Know Which Landing Page Is Working Best? Do I Have Enough Clicks To Tell Yet?
    • When Should I Stop Running A Campaign Or An Offer?
    • I've Got A Working Campaign! How Do I Scale It Up?
    • How Do I Scale Up To Making $x,xxx A Day Or More?
