    Eight figures and counting...I'm going off the rails on a crazy train!

    I made it. I really have. H.O.L.Y S.H.I.T!!!

    I have endured the pain and now I can enjoy the gain. I wanted to put this up before the meetup to motivate you to attend. Though, it took me a while but it is definitely worth it in the end, just believe that. Last time we agreed when meet I should be making 500k right? Yeah! This now seems real as am hitting the high six figures at the moment and have made well past eight figures in revenue.

    I am not going to go into the nitty gritty of what I do to make this money. It’s rather obvious, isn’t it? I have come to reassure you people that if you treat AM like a real business, the profits would be real, surreal! If you are as broke as a church rat, just know that I was the same way not a long time ago.

    I have compiled a list of cliches that WORK, work like charm. You would have heard these things umpteen times, but I am amazed how people are afraid of implementing such simple things. So, here you go:

    List of Internet Marketing Cliches That Work:

    1. NEVER BE AFRAID OF FAILURE. it’s just an opportunity for me to begin again in a clever and smarter way. Think about it.

    2. Hard work is important, but attention to detail is where the wealth lies: I owe this one to my business partner Mike. Do things, twice, thrice, whatever. Make sure it not only gets done, but gets done the right way. Don't half-arse things. Make sure to make sure that everything is done with perfection. Ask people again and again. Most people are incompetent. You are not!

    3. Consistency is way more important than “crests and thoughts”: You know what I mean here. Don’t get excited and do a bunch of super-hero-esque shit you can’t repeat everyday. The ability to remain consistent with the business issue is key to success. My strategy has been continuous KEEP AT IT until I got where I am. The idea is to throw shit at the wall every single day, until it starts sticking. It will, it did for me. It did for a bunch of people in here. YOU ARE GOOD ENOUGH.


    4. "MOST IMPORTANT" Get a business partner: Let's face it, a lot of stuff in this business is monotonous and boring. My motivation level is sky high when both Mike and myself are working together. It all comes down to motivation. It's similar to having a workout partner. If you see your partner is achieving gains it makes you want to emulate their success. Together you help each other find ways to get to the next level..whether its optimising nutrition, training, or intensity. You have to really trust your business partner. They need to be your friend. If I can give you some advice that will really take you to the next level. Find that person who you like hanging around outside of business...that is REALLY GOOD AT THIS BUSINESS. Offer value and hard work. Make yourself a better person and the people you want to attract will come to you. This is not a surprise, Napoleon Hill figured it out decades ago. It’s your time now. MASTERMIND!

    I will leave you with this:
    Unless you are willing to drench yourself in your work beyond the capacity of the average man, you are just not cut out for positions at the top.
    See you all at the meetup on Dec. 7, 2015. I will be there to meet you.

    This is one account on one network and we have multiple of both. You know what I mean. You are smart people.

STM is the benchmark, the pre-requisite if you wan to succeed in IM, as far as I am concerned. I like to call it the "Saul Goodman" of Internet marketing. They always know a guy, who knows a guy, who knows another guy....you get the picture
