    Originally Posted by mantas
    Don't want to hate but it has given 0 value to me. It has been a one big pitch fest. Honestly, Vortex's 40-day tutorial is 1000x more valuable.
    I have 2 questions for you:
    - what were you waiting for? If it was step-by-step campaigns, then that was probably the wrong event. There was an event earlier this year, during which twinaxe detailed push strategies for one hour, that was much more suitable for beginners.
    - did you have a theme you wanted to focus on? I understand that if you watched the full 3 days, you may have had that feeling. At the beginning, I asked their support if they had presentations in PDF. They sent a folder with all presentations. I was able to skim through all presentations and eliminate around 50%. Then I eliminated ecom / podcasting / influencers / SEO / amazon / agencies, and I was left with around 10-15 conf.

    I found some interesting one like
    - Kris Sugatan - How to Build a High-Performance Team With Less than 1% Turnover Rate
    - Justin Brooke - How To Generate 10,000 Leads a Day With Google Ads
    - Natasha Takahashi - Generate Immediate ROI with a Chatbot

    Worst for me was Daniel Hernandez - Leveraging AI to Uncover Your Companies' Weaknesses. Pure corporate BS without any value.

    My 2 cents for future editions:
    - avoid .KEY as the format to download the presentations. Some villains like me still use Windows, and it is a pain in the ass to have to convert each presentation from KEY to PDF. Hopefully the support was reactive in sending all PDFs.
    - have a video player that allows x2 speed when watching recorded. It was avaible at the begining, but I guess you had to change platform during day 1.

    Not so much true "affiliate" content IMHO, but then it's called "Adworld" and not "Affiliate World", so I get it, the focus is on much broader audience than affiliates.

    There are definitely some good ones and even if there is only 1 takeaway from those 100 of speeches, it's worth it.

    Some I liked so far (though it's all about what are you looking for):
    - Amazon Secrets for 2021: Traffic, Attribution and Ranking Techniques by Zack Franklin
