    NEW STM Live Training: How To Get The Most From STM

    Hey everyone!

    Longtime members may have noticed that I've been a bit quiet on the site of late. That's because the STM Ruling Cabal set me on a secret mission, and you're about to see the fruits of it.

    What was the secret mission?

    Live training at STM.



    Yes, we're leaping forward to the bleeding edge of teaching - we'll be running live streaming video courses on AM in 2015!

    And the first one - the alpha test - is scheduled for this Monday



    Getting The Most From STM

    Our first live training session is aimed at newcomers to STM, although more experienced members may pick up a tip or three too.

    I'll take you through all the tricks to get the most out of your STM subscription:

    • How best to use the site and everything we have to offer to skyrocket yourself to profitability.
    • The tricks us mods use to keep up with every single conversation on the site.
    • Ways to mine the past for golden info that other affiliates miss.
    • How, where and what to ask to get all your questions answered quickly.
    • How to run a Follow-Along that gives you the answers you need to improve.


    And more!

    (In fact, if there's anything you'd like me to cover in the session, mention it below - or you could just ask when we're live!)

    I'll be running that session on Monday 1st December, at three times:

    • 1:00pm GMT - perfect for evening viewing in East Asia and early evening in India.
    • 6:30pm GMT - the Eurozone-friendly timing.
    • 11:30pm GMT - yes, I'm running a night owl session for US affiliates (and night owls or early risers elsewhere)!


    You'll need to sign up in advance for the sessions: visit

    training.stmforum.com

    to sign up!



    Notes

    As I mentioned above, if there's anything you want me to cover in particular, do mention it below! We'll have live chat running during the session so you can talk to me, though, so don't worry if you forget something.

    This is an "alpha test" session - the first one we've ever run. We've put a lot of time into making sure that the technology's solid, but still, there are likely to be bugs! Apologies for that in advance, and if you get caught by one of them, please do let support know at http://support.stmforum.com - they'll be standing by ready to fix any problems.

    The signup site works on mobile, tablet and desktop, but the streaming site may have bugs on mobile and tablet. Feel free to try joining from your iPad or your iPhone - and let us know if you have problems - but if you have trouble a desktop is likely to be your best bet.

    Obviously, you'll also need reasonably stable internet access to view the streaming video!



    See you on Monday, and if you've got any questions, comments or whatever, post 'em below! Exciting times!
