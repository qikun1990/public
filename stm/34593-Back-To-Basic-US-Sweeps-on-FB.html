    Back To Basic - US Sweeps on FB

    Struggling as a "newbie" becoming profitable? Let's take it to the basics then!

    Where it's quite normal and usual to avoid starting at a Tier-1 country as a newbie, especially not the US, here is a "simple" guide to reach 100% ROI, without using any software, platforms, cloaking, spy tools or what so ever. All ya need is some budget to invest on Facebook! And ofcourse a Facebook account which is ready to enter the battle!

    Campaign details:
    Geo: US
    Campaign: Sweepstake - Supermarket X (there are dozens, let's take Publix as example)
    Traffic source: Facebook
    Payout: 2 dollar
    Tracking: Hasoffers dashboard of the network/advertiser

    Targeting setup:
    - United States
    - Targeting - women, 35+ who liked Publix
    - Mobile newsfeed only
    - Automatic bidding

    Ad setup:

    - Find images of Publix stores, Publix bags, Publix cart's etc etc. Don't use a logo only, but find some nice HQ images.

    - Create a FB page, which makes it look like you are somehow affiliated with Publix. How aggressive you want to go is up to you, from "Publix Stores" to "Pub Stores". The more convincing, the better the CTR and CR will be eventually. But beware of copyright infringement and that kind of stuff. Don't harm the brand at least, keep it positive!

    - Use a simple header as; Ladies, Attention! We Might Have A Shopping Treat For You! #giftcard

    - Use a simple call to action underneath the image like; Click Here & Proceed
    *always let the advertiser check if the text you are using is allowed

    - Ofcourse use the visible link/link description part for trustworthy text, call to actions, user reviews etc etc

    You might be able to direct link (if the lander contains some questions before submit page) or create a simple 4-question prelander yourself.

    With this setup, (my first steps in the US market too!) I got these results;


    My ads reach a nice average of 0,12 euro ct. Not amazing but decent for a first try!

    Good luck!
