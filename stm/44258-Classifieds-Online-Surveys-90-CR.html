    Classifieds + Online Surveys = 90% CR

    Today I want to share a quickie with you that I posted on my blog few days ago.

    ItÂ´s no long story and nothing spectacular.

    But it shows that you can have good success when you get little bit creative with what you do.

    Few years ago I was running some campaigns for online survey offers.

    As traffic I used classified traffic.

    I ran the campaigns in Canada so I used Kijiji to post my ads there.

    First I tested with free ads but the problem was that they were pushed down pretty fast when new ads were posted.

    Then I ran the campaigns with paid ads and it worked very good. (EDIT BY VORTEX: This is important! Most classified ads are strict about spam, so paid traffic may be your best bet. Please see this post: https://stmforum.com/forum/showthrea...l=1#post371259)

    The Ad Setup

    I donÂ´t have the exact text anymore but it was something like
    âWe pay for your opinion.
    We work with big companies who wants to know what you think about their productsâ.

    It was just a rather short text with a link to my landing page.

    I posted it in âPart Time & Studentsâ jobs.

    Price was about $25 or so per ad for a week and I just posted in the biggest cities and regions.

    Mostly after some time my account got banned so I just opened a new one with new VCC and continued.

    The Landing Page

    The landing page itself also was very simple, basically just telling that we work together with the biggest and most reliable companies.

    These companies are interested in getting opinions and that they pay for it ($75-$100/Day possible).

    Then there was a comparison of three different offers, all rated 5 starts and with few positive bullet points.

    In the end it was mostly only one offer that converted really good so I could just have that one offer on the lander.

    But tests showed that conversions are better when users have more offers to chose from.

    With a good offer these campaigns converted VERY good.

    I had up to 90% conversion rate and also never had quality issues with the network.

    The campaigns made good $XXX/Day in revenue so it was pretty easy and nice money.

    As stupid as it sounds but I just stopped with it because I got bored from doing it.

    I hope you enjoyed this little story, will post more of it later.
