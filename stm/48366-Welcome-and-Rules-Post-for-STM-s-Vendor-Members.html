    Welcome and Rules Post for STM's Vendor Members

    (If you haven't already, please read the welcome post for all new members before reading this one.)

    Hello Vendor Members! Allow me once again to welcome you to STM!

    (And if you haven't already, please read this welcome post for all new members, in addition to the current post.)

    We have many vendors here on STM, including representatives from traffic sources and affiliate networks, and reps/owners of other affiliate marketing related products and services. We're very glad to have you join us!

    This post will provide valuable tips on how to maximize exposure for your business without breaking forum rules. So please do read it to the end.



    Forum Rules for Vendors

    1)Please carefully review our forum rules for vendors. (Note: This is in addition to our rules for all members mentioned in the other welcome post.)


    2)If you have a bonus or discount to offer to fellow STM members (thank you in advance!), you can create a thread in the STM Member Discounts and Bonuses subforum.


    3)Every Affiliate Network or Traffic Network is entitled to making one official network thread in this section:

    https://stmforum.com/forum/forumdisp...etwork-Threads

    You can post anything you like to promote your business (as long as it's legal and doesn't break other forum rules). You may add replies to the thread for up to 2 times a week with any new information, such as offers etc.


    4)If you are an affiliate network with unique offer opportunities, or a traffic network with unique traffic opportunities, you can post them in Affiliate Offers & Traffic Opportunities.


    5)If you have exclusive offers or exclusive traffic opportunities (exclusive meaning something ONLY your network has), you can post them in Exclusive Offer Opportunities.


    6)If you have a product or service to sell/promote that isn't an affiliate offer or traffic opportunity, please post in the STM Marketplace subforum.


    7)Please do not send unsolicited PMs to members! You can only PM a member about your products/services if s/he has explicitly expressed interest in the type of products/services you're selling, or have asked to be contacted by a rep from your company.


    8)Please make sure that whatever you're selling is legal - nothing that would get our members in legal trouble if they buy your product or use your service.

    Also, if you're selling accounts (here's the section to do this in), make sure they're not hacked or stolen accounts.

    We also prohibit the buying/selling of Facebook and Google ad accounts (please find details here).


    9)As a vendor, if you wish to contribute a case study or a post from your company blog, please only post in the Vendor Case Studies and Blog Posts section.

    Please only post useful content. If reposting a blog post, please include the ENTIRE blog post, and not just a summary with a link to the original post. When reposting a blog post in its entirety, you are allowed to include, in the post body, ONE link to the original blog post. This is the only exception to the "no promotional links allowed in the post body" rule.

    ***************

    Each network may start a maximum of ONE self-promotional thread each WEEK in only ONE of the subforums listed above.

    With the exception of the Official Network Thread (which can be bumped for up to 2 times/week), please do not bump your threads except to answer member questions.

    Please do not promote your offers/products/services in threads outside of the ones mentioned above! Doing so would be considered spamming and can result in an account ban. We take spamming VERY seriously.

    ***************

    Again, for detailed forum rules for vendors, please see here.



    Other Ways to Get More Exposure

    1)Please add a forum signature by clicking on "Settings" at the top right-hand corner, then clicking on "Edit Signature" in the left sidebar.

    In the signature you can write a very brief intro of your product/service, along with a link (can be an affiliate link).


    2)Participate in forum discussions in a helpful and informative way! Please do NOT self-promote in threads outside of the exceptions mentioned above, unless another STM member has explicitly asked for recommendations for your type of product/service.

    The more you post, the more people will see your forum signature. Also, the more helpful you are, the more members will like you and your business. Lots of vendors have built very good reputation just by doing this.

    However, please do not make posts that are not useful, just to get eyeballs on your signature. This would be spamming which is against forum rules.


    3)If you represent an affiliate network, and it is newbie-friendly, you can consider participating in this tool: https://stmforum.com/central-vendor-app/

    (If interested, please shoot an email to support@stmforum.com to ask for my contact info.)


    4)If your business offers bonuses or discounts, here's the section to post about it:

    https://stmforum.com/forum/forumdisp...ts-amp-Bonuses


    5)If your network ever wants to run a contest, here's the section to post about it:

    https://stmforum.com/forum/forumdisp...liate-Contests


    6)We have an Affiliate Contacts Directory you can add an entry to for your company: https://stmforum.com/affiliate-directory/


    7)We have paid advertising, in the form of a sponsored listing in both our weekly newsletter and the MRKTRS newsletter. To see examples, you can find back issues of our newsletter at https://stmforum.com/blog. If you would like more information, please email support@stmforum.com to ask for my contact info.



    ***************

    Thank you very much for taking the time to read the above!

    We're always looking for more ideas to create more value for our members! If you have ideas on how we can collaborate to create win-win-wins for our members, STM, and your business, please don't hesitate to get in touch with support@stmforum.com with your proposal, and we'll talk!



    Amy
