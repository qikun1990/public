    Superbowl VS Valentines Day $$$ (2 Profiting Campaigns Inside!)

    *Just a heads up, this guide is conjumbled with a lot of confusion. That's because there's a lot of opportunities here, and i wanted to fit everything in. In the guide you will find 2 campaigns that can profit $1000/day each, and 1 campaign that is IMPOSSIBLE not to profit from. So if your looking to get your first profiting campaign up, look for the 'Superbowl Email submit PPV guide below!'

    > Superbowl VS Valentines Day
    The Superbowl is in 7 days, and Valentines day is in 14 days. Both can make you a ton of $ but I've always made more with the Superbowl. Back in the day 'Crush Mobile offers' was the way i used to make money during Valentines day, but it's not the same anymore (but don't count it out completely -- there are still tons of Mobile Crush offers converting at amazing numbers). Most of valentines day offers are Cost Per Sale, which are harder to convert for most people.

    The following are Valentines day offers you could run in 2011:
    The offers below are CPA offers
    - Valentines Day $100 Teleflora Gift Card - Email Submit
    - $100 Victoria's Secret Valentines GC - Email Submit (Exclusive)
    - Free Roses for Valentines Day $100 Teleflora Gift Card
    - Crush Mobile offers (too many to name!)
    (Everything above SCREAMS PPV!!!)

    3 Offers below are CPS offers, found on the AKMG Affiliate Network
    - FTD - Valentine’s Day Flowers (Pays: $16 on any purchase from website)
    - Red Envelope (= Valntines day gift store) (Pays: $12 on any purchase from website)
    - Shari’s Berries (=Chocolate berries and such) (Pays: $11.25 on any purchase from website)
    (As you can see the Cost Per Sale offers are pretty good, but you need the right campaign to sell these)

    > My 2 Superbowl Campaigns
    Both these work year round, but do better during Superbowl time! (Get them up now, and keep them going)

    1. Superbowl/NFL Email Submits (PPV)
    For those of you who are still trying to get a campaign going. This is a campaign that you ABSOLUTELY CANNOT FAIL WITH!
    The campaign overall is targeting NFL URLs/Keywords with a NFL related landing page asking a question and offering up an email submit prize.

    Some general notes before i go into detail
    - I've personally never used keywords before for this campaign, only scraped URLs.
    - The NFL.com and the Superbowl related targets have some MASSIVE amounts of traffic this week.
    - You HAVE to mix up your email submits, your conversion rates will drop fast. Have a big rotation of many email submits and drop the ones that have shitty conversion rates.
    - Be controversial on the landing page, this goes for a lot of affiliate marketing campaigns!
    - If your one of the first people to get this up, or just don't have a lot of competition you can make $1000/day running this campaign -- as long as it's broken down across many tighter niche groups. Meaning instead of throwing all NFL URLs into 1 campaign with 1 landing page, you separate them the way I'll be explaining below.
    - I spent $204 and made $687 in 2 days from this campaign!


    a) Targeting Superbowl/NFL as a whole:
    (This is how i made the $687 in the last 2 days, and how i made a lot more last year)
    - Landing page:
    "Who's Going To Win The Superbowl",
    "Who's Going To Win The Superbowl XLV",
    "Did The Packers Already Win The Superbowl?" (see what i did there), -- Have your titles be confusing, it will only make you more money. Put a picture of each team, and buttons for each.
    - URLs/Keywords: Anything Superbowl/NFL related

    b) Targeting Specific NFL teams:
    - Landing page:
    "Should The Cowboys Be In The Superbowl?",
    "Who's Going To Win? Packers or Cowboys?", etc…
    - URLs/Keywords: Target a specific team, make sure you have a LOT of longtail URLs, don't just target dallascowboys.com
    - For this campaign you can target the 2 teams in the superbowl with a title like the 2nd one, or target other teams with the title like the 1st one above.

    c) Targeting NFL players:
    Follow the same as above!

    *There's a lot of options and ways to diversify yourself from the rest. Get on this ASAP!

    -----

    2. NFL/Superbowl Dating
    Think along the lines of:
    "Meet A Superbowl Cutie", "Meet An NFL Beauty"
    This campaign isn't a must during the Superbowl, but the CTR does go up when you create ads directly related to the Superbowl.
    Traffic Sources: Facebook & POF
    - POF has some great targeting options for this!

    a) Targeting NFL as a whole:
    - 1st way to do this is to target general football. It's the easiest way to get approved too. Use 'http://www.fitness-singles.com' as your fake landing page to get approved. Fitness Singles doesn't have an offer, otherwise I'd suggest you use that as your actual offer. Just use whatever dating offers are at the top of your affiliate network at the time and split test a few of them. Also if you do find any fitness related dating offers, throw those in the mix.
    - Keywords: NFL, NFL teams, NFL players, Dallas Cowboys, Superbowl ...ANYTHING NFL related!
    - There are '7,116,280' single men, who are 25+, looking for women in the United States. If your target the parameters above and every NFL keyword on Facebook, you will be targeting over 5,500,000 of those men. That's 70% of the single male population in the United States on Facebook! I know this because i spent over 10 hours gathering keywords!
    - Use a landing page, relate it to football obviously. (The perks of having a GF who likes Football.. etc..)

    b) Targeting NFL teams:
    - 2nd way to do this is to target specific football teams. "Girls Love Denver Broncos" = Ad Title. Target all 'Denver Bronco' keywords, follow the same guidelines above and your set. Only problem is, it's hard to get this one approved. What to do? Well it's going to take you many times to get approved that's first. Make a landing page related to the Denver Bronco's and the dating offer your promoting -- that will help a bit. Still expect many disapproval notices.

    Dating is a very touchy subject on Facebook and be prepared to see a LOT of disapproved ads. If your persistent and have time to do this, this campaign is great and has a lot of room to expand.

    Offers that work with the male football crowd? Think the opposite of 'relationship' -- 1 night stand offers, russian datings offers etc.. These are the offers Facebook doesn't like, so be sure to use a dating offer allowed by Facebook to get approved, then swap your offers. Facebook likes: Fitness Singles, Match.com, etc.. (anything that focuses on relationships)

    Follow these guidelines for dating or you WILL NOT get approved for sure.
    (saving you time here!)


    More Offers (Superbowl Related Offers)
    - FREE: GoFreeGifts - SuperBowl - 60in. HDTV
    - Superbowl Promotion - HDTV Giveaway (EMAIL SUBMIT)
    - FREE: SurveyThis - NFL Superbowl: Who are you rooting for?
    > Just go to http://affbuzz.com/offerbuzz/ and search "superbowl"

    Moving The Campaigns To Other Sports
    - I've tried only with the NBA and NHL for PPV/submits.
    - I noticed less traffic for both and conversion rates were way down for NHL.
    - NBA/NHL dating campaigns look promising. I'll be trying this myself very soon. Also International dating with the country's your targeting top sports. (That's a whole new realm of affiliate marketing... this idea just came to me and it's going on my important to-do list)
