    My stable year - 7 figures a month!

    Hi guys,

    For you of those I don’t know, my name is Hen Kinan and I have been a member on STM since 2014.

    I have been an affiliate for the past 6-7 years, in different niches; from toolbars to E-com, to mobile content and CPLs. But, as things tend to do in this industry, one day, I realized that things have gotten a little too aggressive for my tastes.

    We’ve all been there. We used to make thousands of dollars, just to realize the traffic we sent is fraud & get a huge charge-back. (yea ok, we used to see that the conversion rate is a bit high but both us and the advertiser/affiliate network ignored it)
    After few months like that, it felt like we were chasing our own tail, and so, 2+ years ago we decided to come up with a solution that would be more sustainable and reliable. From an “easy money now” mindset we decided to move to a long-term operation.

    Just like everybody else, we were using spy tools to get a better understanding of the market. We would get all sorts of results, and just like everybody else, one type of result we always ignored was viral content. It was literality everywhere we looked
    That line of thought led us to found SHINEz.

    We used all our knowledge as affiliates (A lot of it thanks to STM & AWC) to start building this, connected dots and added a strong tech team.
    My team is build from 24 employees in Tel-Aviv, 20 content writers WW & 3 developer in Russia. (For the moment
    We are generating 7 figures a month for some times now, all white-hat and big % on Facebook.

    SHINEz, the company we’ve built over the past two years, offers powerful new tools which allow affiliates like us to generate profit just by promoting viral content.
    On this AWE Barcelona I’m going to be presenting a few concrete case studies, with real numbers, and hope to let people in on the huge potential this vertical offers. If you’re looking to make profit of viral content, I think my speech will be a game-changer for you.

    See you all soon!!!

    Cheers,
    Kinan
