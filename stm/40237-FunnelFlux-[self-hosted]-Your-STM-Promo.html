    UPDATE

    We have now launched FunnelFlux Pro, our SaaS solution.

    Check it out here, including your super awesome discounts - https://stmforum.com/forum/showthrea...-for-6-months)









    Hey!

    Zeno here, long time STM Forum contributor and now the CEO of FunnelFlux.

    In this thread I'd like to introduce FunnelFlux, the promo we are offering to STM Forum members, and a quick bullet point list of why on earth you should use FunnelFlux.

    If you are not familiar with FunnelFlux, it's a tracking system for performance marketing that gives you a huge amount of control over your campaigns while taking a very visual approach to configuring your tracking.

    FunnelFlux is a venture brought to you by the team at iStack that runs STM Forum, Affiliate World Conferences, iStack Training, Adplexity and many other ventures (so you know you're in good hands).

    It's the tracking system we fully endorse at STM.








    What is the goal of FunnelFlux?

    Well, rather than configuring row after row of rules, lists of landers, offers, etc., we want you to be able to build everything in a visual flow-chart approach -- the way marketers think and want to describe how their traffic should flow.




    You can see more here - https://funnelflux.com/self-hosted


    What exactly does FunnelFlux offer?

    • The most important aspect as I mentioned above is that you can visually build complex and powerful funnels without having to do any difficult coding. Need a quick video? Check out my 10 min intro here - https://youtu.be/mh0TH2apE0M
    • Want to build a funnel like this? It's easy






    • There are no limits on entry points. Most trackers have one campaign links that traffic goes to. With FunnelFlux, you can tell traffic to start at any point in your funnel. See the funnel above? I can easily make a link that skips the initial iOS/US condition and goes straight to my upsell/cross-sell path. No need to make an entirely new funnel or campaign
    • The visual heatmap views let you see your traffic flow and key metrics in a visual way. Sick of tables of data? Heatmaps can let you visualise EPV across your funnel.
    • We have normal reporting as well, which allows deeper drilldowns than any other tracker and without limitation. Need to drill down by 10 different parameters? No problem.
    • We use DigitalElement for mobile carrier/ISP detection, giving you industry-leading accuracy for these attributes
    • Our reporting provides you easy statistical indicators for winning CTR, CVR and EPV metrics
    • Multiple redirect modes available for landers and offers, including an advanced "ultimate meta refresh" as well as our custom "Fluxify" technology that lets you dynamically scrape, modify and test landers in real-time that you don't even own!
    • You can use no-redirect JS tracking so that you can send traffic direct to landers, no need to go through the tracker first - great for running high volume pops with landers on CDNs and when running FB/Google/Bing traffic in cases you want to avoid redirects. Other trackers may offer "LP pixels" but we're also adding many features to the javascript itself, as well as a Javascript API
    • Case in point, our on-page JS tracking lets you easily measure time on page, an extremely useful and underutilised metric for optimising on native traffic
    • We provide advanced multivariate testing using JS and PHP - for those of you who want to get into nitty gritty optimisation
    • You can track lifetime value of leads across different funnels and connect with email activity - so you can always see the overall revenue from your traffic. If you're doing email follow ups you can connect the dots and attribute downstream conversions to your original ads
    • We have added features that make tracking work correctly in situations where browsers are blocking cookies - something which seems to have been lost in recent years as trackers became more user-friendly. Running complex iFramed landers on Facebook mobile and getting weird issues with data getting lost? No longer a problem.
    • You can point an unlimited number of domains at your tracker, they will all work. You can even create dynamic links and landing pages so that you can change domains on the fly and not need to update links in any of your pages. Great if you are constantly needing to switch things up, e.g. if domains are being flagged our you want to have a low footprint by using many domains


    On a more technical note:

    • The software is self-hosted, so if you are doing volume e.g. pops, your tracking can be much cheaper than SaaS options
    • We have a deployment manager that makes it easy to install the software on several recommended VPS providers
    • We also have a managed version (see promo below) for those of you who want a higher performance system without having to deal with the technicalities of servers yourself
    • Lastly, we have a small development team but we move quickly and are constantly adding new features, improvements and polish. You won't find another tracker that moves as fast as we do, nor will you find a team that has such a strong understanding of tracking in affiliate marketing -- FunnelFlux is lead by myself and a lead developer who eats, sleeps and breathes tracking, and is constantly creating new and exciting ways to get better at it.


    So with that summary out of the way...



    What do I get as an STM Forum member?

    For our self-hosted tracker, we'll give you 10% off the monthly price i.e. $89/mo instead of $99/mo. Like our $1 trial, this is only available to new customers (sorry veterans, we still love you tho).

    You can take advantage of this promo here - https://funnelflux.com/partner/stm/

    If you are looking for a SaaS/Cloud tracker instead, we highly recommend that you check out FunnelFlux Pro, our next generation tracker -- https://funnelflux.com/pro/



    Cheers!

    Create as in make them from scratch? No, we don't have any sort of in built landing page creator.

    However, we do have a feature called "Fluxify" which lets you take the URL of any lander, i.e. a lander being used by another marketer, then clone it, modify it and replace links in it -- all on the fly and without having to download and edit the lander yourself (it hosts it on your tracker).

    We will be updating this feature soon to also locally cache static assets like CSS, image etc. as well to make it even more robust.
